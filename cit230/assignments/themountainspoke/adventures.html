Adventures

Grand Loop

Distance: 10 mi

Estimated Time: 2 hours

Frequency: 10 am and 3 pm everyday

Skill Level: Beginner

Cost: $25

The Grand Loop takes riders on the Booze Mountain trail to enjoy the scenery of Bozeman. This is perfect for those looking into mountain biking or those just starting out. Children above the age of ten are welcome. The tour starts at our Bozeman store location.

Mountain Trek

Distance: 20 mi

Estimated Time: 3 hours

Frequency: 12pm everyday

Skill Level: Intermediate

Cost: $50

The Forrest Trek takes riders through the forrested mountains of Bozeman where they will switch between trails and free riding. Intermediate riders and above only. This trail will help riders improve their ability to navigate difficult terrain. Bikers will meet at our Bozeman store location.
